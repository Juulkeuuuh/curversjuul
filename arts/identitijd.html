IDENTITIJD

Identitijd is a collaborative project between Maastricht Museum and Zuyd Hogeschool.

Together with Iris van Luinen, we developed this project from the ground up.

Concept

Identitijd was created to give every child a sense of recognition and belonging within the museum. By creating their own Identitijdskaart, they become part of the museum, almost like a researcher or staff member.

Interactive Website

We designed a website where children could create their personal Identitijdskaart.

Note: The website was designed for a Dutch-speaking audience.

Educational Materials

The project includes printed materials for teachers to guide students through the process.

Bringing Stories to the Museum

After completing their short story (max. 2 minutes), children record it via Vocaroo, generating a unique QR code.

This QR code is printed on a custom-designed frame, which can be placed in the museum using magnets. This allows children to physically place their story next to their chosen object, making their contribution a real part of the exhibition.
