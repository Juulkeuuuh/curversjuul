SONAYOU - BB

For this project, the artist Sonayou approached me with the request to create a cover for her single BB.

She provided a mood board and some sketches, and I translated her vision into a 3D-rendered design using Blender.

In addition to the cover art, this project also included animations for Spotify and a music video.

Spotify Animation

The animation was designed to loop seamlessly on Spotify, creating a dynamic visual experience for listeners.

Color & Style Exploration

During the design process, I explored different color variations and compositions to achieve the right look.

Final Animations

These final animated sequences were used for the promotional video and visual identity of the single.
