BRASSA

Brassa is a brasserie in Lanaken that opened on September 1st, 2023.

Concept

The client wanted a luxurious identity that fit the location. The name "Brassa" is derived from the word "embrace."

Branding

I started by designing an icon that reflects Brassa's identity.

Once the icon was finalized, I focused on typography. Helvetica was chosen for its modern, recognizable style, aligning with the brand's elegant and high-end feel.

Website Design

I designed, developed, and launched a website for Brassa.
