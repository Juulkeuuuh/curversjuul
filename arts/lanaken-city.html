LANAKEN CITY

Lanaken City is a project where I was tasked with designing a Monopoly-inspired board game.

Concept

The client requested a design that incorporated a skyline and local photography. I handled the entire graphic design, including the box, game board, chance cards, money, and instruction booklet.

Production

The game is based on the town of Lanaken, Belgium, and was produced in a limited run of 300 editions.

Branding & 3D Renders

The project began with designing a logo and establishing a cohesive visual style.

Through continuous communication, I developed 3D renders to visualize the final product.

Final Product

After finalizing all files for print production, we successfully created a fully designed board game.
