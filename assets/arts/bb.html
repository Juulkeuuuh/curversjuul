SONAYOU - BB

For this project, the artist //Sonayou// came to me with the request to create a cover for her single 'BB'.

She gave me a mood, and some sketches and I opened up Blender to realize her vision.

This project also came with animations for spotify and a video clip.

The spotify animation is made to loop.
