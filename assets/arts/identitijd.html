IDENTITIJD

Identitijd is a project in collaboration with Maastricht Museum and Zuyd Hogeschool.

Together with Iris van Luinen we made this project from scratch.

Maastricht Museum gave us the request to make an educational program for kids arount the age of 11/12.

Identitijd Maastricht is a website we created where kids can make their own identity card. We want each kid to feel like they are part of the museum.

Keep in mind it's for a Dutch audience.

The whole site comes with papers for the teachers.

Kids have to chose their favorite object in the online archive of the museum and need to make a short story around it.

On the same website you can find a page of information for every object.

After making a short story (max 2 mins) the kids can record it on the website Vocaroo. It gives them a QR-code which can be pasted on to a pdf we give them. After printing it out they can put it in to a special box at the museum and put it next to their chosen object. Now they are part of the museum
