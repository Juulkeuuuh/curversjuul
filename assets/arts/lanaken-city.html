LANAKEN CITY

This is a project where I was asked to create a (monopoly based) game.

The client's requirement was to work with a skyline and photos. The entire graphic design comes from my hand. The design comes in the form of a box, a game board, chance cards, money and a game explanation.

The game is based on the village of Lanaken (Belgium) and has sold 300 editions.

I started by making a logo and comcepting an over all style:

Then by communicating a lot I made up 3D renders.

All the files to the printing office, and we got an fully designed boards game!
