BRASSA

Brassa is a new brasserie in Lanaken which openend on september first 2023.

The owners wanted something luxurious that would also fit in the location.

Brassa is a translation from the word embrace.

I started with creating an icon.

After the icon I added the text.

I chose Helvetica because it is well known by Apple users which can be related to a more luxurious feel.

I made a website.

I designed it, coded it and put it online.
